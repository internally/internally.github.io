How Can InternAlly Help You?

What is InternAlly?

The Covid-19 pandemic has left many college students without summer internships. Simultaneously, startups continue to look for talent to join their teams remotely. This search is often made more difficult for startups due to limited resources for recruiting.

InternAlly is a startup that bridges the gap between students looking for remote summer work and startups looking for fresh talent to join them. At InternAlly, we match students and startups so that students can find meaningful summer work and startups can build and grow during these tough times.

How InternAlly Works

Students

Step 1: Fill out the student qualifications & interests survey (you will need to upload your resume and transcript so be sure to have those ready). Once we receive your application we will look for startups that may be a match for your skills and interests.

Step 2: Get matched with a startup looking for a student with your qualifications and working on a project that aligns with your interests.

Step 3: Wait for the startup team to reach out so you can get started!

Startups

Step 1: Fill out the startup profile to help us understand what type of student you are looking for.

Step 2: Receive resumes and transcripts from multiple students who fit your profile!

Step 3: Choose the student that aligns best with your needs and contact them at your own pace!

What types of opportunities are available for students?

We are partnering with many different types of startups to have a variety of opportunities for students. Opportunities may be part-time or full-time internships, and they may be paid or unpaid jobs. They can range from summer research opportunities, to short-term gigs, to permanent positions.

Am I guaranteed to get a match?

The matching process depends on the positions available from startups, as well as the skill sets and interests of students. After you apply, a match is not guaranteed, but we will do our best to connect students and startups that are a good fit for each other!
