HOW IT WORKS

How Can InternAlly Help You?

What is InternAlly?

InternAlly is a startup that bridges the gap between students looking for remote summer work and startups looking for fresh talent to join them. Due to the Covid-19 pandemic, many students have had difficulties with their summer internships and jobs. On the other hand, startups have had a difficult time recruiting talent to do remote work, due to limited capital and resources to recruit.

At InternAlly, we aim to pair up these students and startups so that students can find meaningful summer work and startups can build and grow during these tough times.

How does it work?

If you are a student, you can begin by filling out a student signup form. Once we receive your application, we will look for startups that may be a match for your skills and interests. If we find a match, we will notify you through the email you provided in your application and put you in touch with the startup's team!

If you are a startup, you can begin by filling out a startup signup form. Once we receive your application, we will look for students that may be a match for your company. If we find a match, we will notify you through the email you provided in your application and put you in touch with the student!

What opportunities are available for students?

We are partnering with many startups to obtain a variety of opportunities for students. Some types of opportunities may be part-time or full-time internships, and they may be paid or unpaid jobs. Furthermore, they can range from summer research opportunities, to short-term gigs, to permanent positions.

Am I guaranteed to get a match?

The matching process depends on the positions available from startups, as well as the skill sets and interests of students. After you apply, a match is not guaranteed, but we will do our best to connect students and startups that are a good fit for each other!

If I have questions or concerns, who do I email?

For any questions or concerns, you can email the InternAlly team at internally@gmail.com.
