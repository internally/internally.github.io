How Can InternAlly Help You?

What is InternAlly?

InternAlly is a startup that bridges the gap between students looking for remote summer work and startups looking for fresh talent to join them. Due to the Covid-19 pandemic, many students have had difficulties with their summer internships and jobs. On the other hand, startups have had a difficult time recruiting talent to do remote work, due to limited capital and resources to recruit.

At InternAlly, we aim to pair up these students and startups so that students can find meaningful summer work and startups can build and grow during these tough times.
